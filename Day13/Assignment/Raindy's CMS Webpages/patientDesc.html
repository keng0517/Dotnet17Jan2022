Patient Authentication

Patient must be verified by using existing username and matched password existed in the clinic management system before proceeding to related functions.

Doctor Listing

All of the existing doctor details in clinic management system will be fully displayed for user recognizing purpose.

Appointment Adding

All required details for booking an appointment must be fully inserted by patient, any mistakes or wrong data input are strictly controlled by the system.

Field Editing

Except of auto generated Appointment ID, patient is able to change the existing appointment details separately by choosing prefer from the options.

Payment Making

Any appointment is having status of Waiting For Payment will be displayed and to be selected by patient. Patient is required to confirm the payment details of the appointment.

Histories Viewing

All of existing appointment records with appointment status Paid and Rejected will be displayed in the history listing. Patient is able to refer back for any purposes in future.
